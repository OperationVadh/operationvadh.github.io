Your Mission

DIRECTIVE 01

Identify Targets

Use OSINT techniques to discover scam websites, phishing operations, fraudulent platforms, and malicious actors operating in the shadows.

Track their digital footprints. Document URLs, hosting details, domain information, and infrastructure. Build comprehensive profiles.
DIRECTIVE 02

Gather Evidence

Collect irrefutable proof. Screenshots, archives, metadata, network maps. Build cases that cannot be ignored or dismissed.

Use every tool available: WHOIS lookups, reverse searches, archive services, network analysis. Leave no stone unturned.
DIRECTIVE 03

Create Awareness

Expose their operations publicly. Create content that educates and warns others. Share using #OperationVadh to build collective knowledge.

Write threads, create videos, publish reports. Make it impossible for them to operate unnoticed.
DIRECTIVE 04

Report Bad Actors

File official reports with hosting providers, domain registrars, abuse databases, and law enforcement. Turn evidence into action.

Contact abuse departments, submit to cybercrime units, alert platform moderators. Make their existence untenable.
DIRECTIVE 05

Coordinate Efforts

Share intelligence with others. Verify findings. Amplify important discoveries. We are stronger when we work together.

Collaborate on investigations. Support fellow researchers. Build a network of digital defenders.
